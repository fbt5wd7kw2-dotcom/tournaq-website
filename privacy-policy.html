TournaQ is a local-first app — your game data stays on your device. We do not operate user accounts, and no app data is transmitted to our servers. This policy explains how we handle the limited information that is processed when you use the app.

🏢 Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this app is:

Martin Adam
Email: team@tournaq.com
App: TournaQ (iOS & Android)

📦 Data We Collect and Process

App data (local storage only):

  • Game scores and match results
  • Team and player names you enter
  • Tournament structures you create
This data is stored exclusively on your device using local on-device storage (Hive). It is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not have access to this locally stored data.

Advertising data (via Google AdMob):

TournaQ displays non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob. TournaQ displays non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may process certain technical and device-related information required for ad delivery and fraud prevention in accordance with Google's own privacy policies. We configure AdMob to serve non-personalized ads by default.

⚖️ Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): For advertising-related data processing by Google AdMob, where consent is requested via the in-app UMP consent dialog.
  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)): For the local storage of app data necessary to provide core app functionality (score tracking, tournament management).

You may withdraw your advertising consent at any time via Contact & About → Privacy Options in the app.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Google AdMob / Google LLC

We use Google AdMob to display non-personalized advertisements. Google acts as an independent data controller for data it processes. Google LLC participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data from apps

No other third-party services are used. External links in the app (e.g. Instagram, feedback form) lead to services with their own independent privacy policies.

🌍 International Data Transfers

The app data you enter is stored locally on your device and never transferred internationally by us.

Google AdMob may transfer advertising-related data to servers in the United States. Google LLC is certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, which provides an adequate level of data protection as recognised by the European Commission.

⏱️ Data Retention

  • Local app data: Retained on your device until you delete it via app settings or uninstall the app. We have no control over or access to this data.
  • Advertising data: Retained by Google according to Google's retention policies.

👤 Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

Right of Access Request a copy of your personal data (Art. 15)
Right to Rectification Correct inaccurate data (Art. 16)
Right to Erasure Request deletion of your data (Art. 17)
Right to Restriction Limit how your data is used (Art. 18)
Right to Portability Receive your data in a portable format (Art. 20)
Right to Object Object to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21)

For local app data, you can exercise most of these rights directly by deleting data within the app or uninstalling it. For advertising data processed by Google, contact Google directly or adjust your device's advertising settings.

To exercise your rights regarding any data we control, contact us at team@tournaq.com.

🔔 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. You may do so in the EU member state of your habitual residence, place of work, or where the alleged infringement occurred.

A list of EU supervisory authorities is available at: edpb.europa.eu

👶 Children's Privacy

TournaQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has been affected, please contact us at team@tournaq.com.
