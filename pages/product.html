TournaQ Detail

Beach Volleyball Scoring Today. Tournament Management Tomorrow.

Product Overview

TournaQ is a beach volleyball scoring application designed to make match management simple, fast, and reliable.

The current release focuses on what matters most during live competition: accurate scoring, intuitive match management, and a user experience that works reliably on mobile devices, even without an internet connection.

TournaQ was created from the practical needs of beach volleyball players, referees, and tournament organizers who require a simple and dependable scoring solution during real events.

While scoring is the current focus, TournaQ is being developed with a broader vision: evolving into a complete platform for tournament and competition management.

What TournaQ Does Today

The current version of TournaQ focuses on live match scoring.

Scoring

  • Beach volleyball match scoring
  • Best-of-three matches
  • Custom scoring formats
  • Undo functionality
  • Side changes

Match Management

  • Team management
  • Player management
  • Match history
  • Multi-language support
  • Offline operation

The application is optimized for quick and reliable use during live matches.

Why Beach Volleyball?

Beach volleyball presents unique challenges for tournament organizers and scorekeepers:

  • Outdoor environments
  • Limited internet connectivity
  • Fast-paced match administration
  • Frequent tournament play
  • Mobile-first usage

TournaQ was designed specifically with these requirements in mind. By focusing on beach volleyball first, the platform can provide a more refined and practical experience before expanding into broader tournament functionality.

The Vision

TournaQ is not intended to remain only a scoring application. The long-term vision is to create a platform that supports the entire competition lifecycle.

Competition Lifecycle

  • Tournament creation
  • Team and player management
  • Match scheduling
  • Live scoring

Competition Data

  • Standings
  • Brackets
  • Competition administration
  • Historical records and statistics

The goal is to bring all tournament-related information into a single, easy-to-use platform.
