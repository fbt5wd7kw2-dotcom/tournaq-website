Tournament Management Still Runs on Paper

Running a beach volleyball tournament still largely happens on paper. Score sheets have to be prepared beforehand, distributed to each court, collected after every match, and manually tallied — a slow, error-prone process that TournaQ already replaces with live digital scoring.

  • Court changes and late cancellations are difficult to communicate on the fly
  • Injuries and team withdrawals require unplanned bracket adjustments
  • Standings, seeding, and double-elimination systems demand complex manual calculations
  • League phases and scramble formats add significant organisational overhead

There is no widely adopted digital platform built specifically for beach volleyball. TournaQ is being built to change that — starting with scoring and growing into a complete tournament management solution.
