About TournaQ

TournaQ is developed as a focused sports management tool for beach volleyball. The current release supports scoring, while the long-term goal is to simplify the entire tournament workflow.

Available Today

TournaQ currently focuses on practical beach volleyball scoring and match organization.

Match Scoring

Keep track of beach volleyball scores in a simple and focused interface.

Teams & Games

Support for organizing teams and games as the foundation for future tournament tools.

Multilingual App

Designed with international use in mind, including English, German and Spanish.

Roadmap

The vision is to evolve from scoring into a complete tournament management platform.
